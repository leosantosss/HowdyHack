How to Play

  1. Click "Play" on the home page to start the game.
  2. Select a question by choosing a category and point value.
  3. Type your answer before the timer runs out.
  4. Earn points for correct answers — lose points for wrong ones.
  5. Try to finish with the highest score on the leaderboard!
